Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ViralAI

How does ViralAI identify viral segments?

ViralAI uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze video content, audio transcripts, viewer engagement patterns, and emotional triggers to identify segments with the highest potential for virality. Our AI has been trained on thousands of viral videos to recognize the characteristics that make content shareable.

How many credits do I need to create a short?

Each short you create costs 1 credit. Free users start with 1000 credits, which means you can create up to 1000 shorts before needing to upgrade to a paid plan.

Can I use ViralAI on any YouTube video?

Yes, ViralAI works with any public YouTube video. However, please ensure you have the appropriate rights to create derivative content from the videos you're using. We recommend using your own content or videos with Creative Commons licenses.

How long does it take to create a short?

The process typically takes 1-3 minutes, depending on the length of the original video and the complexity of the viral segment. Our AI works quickly to analyze, extract, and enhance the content for maximum engagement.

Can I customize the shorts before exporting?

Yes, ViralAI offers customization options including adding subtitles, overlaying trending audio, adjusting the aspect ratio, and adding hook text. Pro and Business users have access to more advanced customization features.

How do I post my shorts to TikTok?

Pro and Business users can connect their TikTok accounts directly to ViralAI and post shorts with a single click. Free users can download their shorts and manually upload them to TikTok or any other platform.
